# Lune — Fire and Lightning (early)

## Win condition

Lune's Act 1 power spike is **Critical Burn** feeding off Fire stains. Open with a Fire stain, spam Electrify to build AP, and finish with either a Rockslide break or a Lightning Dance nuke — both are AoE so they clear trash turns fast.

## Rotation

| Turn | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | *Immolation* or *Wildfire* — apply Fire stain → enable Critical Burn trigger |
| 2+ | *Electrify* spam for AP economy |
| burst | *Rockslide* to break armour, *Lightning Dance* to nuke |

## Stat priority

**Might > Luck > Agility**. Might scales her elemental skills, Luck amplifies Critical Burn procs, Agility covers turn order.

## Variants

- Swap *Earth Rising* for *Mayhem* if you don't have the Earth tree unlocked
- *Thunderfall* can replace *Lightning Dance* if it drops first

# Maelle — Pre-Sword Ballet (early)

## Win condition

Before you get Sword Ballet, *Guard Down* is Maelle's best opener — a 25 % all-sources damage debuff that the whole party benefits from. Percée is your consistent damage tool until Fleuret Fury takes over mid-Act-1.

## Rotation

| Turn | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | *Guard Down* — apply 25 % take-damage debuff |
| 2+ | *Percée* for consistent damage; switch stances with *Swift Stride* and *Offensive Switch* |
| later | Once *Fleuret Fury* is online, it outscales Percée on single targets |

## Stat priority

**Might > Agility > Luck**. Might for damage, Agility for stance-swap tempo and crit, Luck for the Critical Burn triggers.

## Variants

- Swap *Medalum* for *Brulerum* if you roll it first
- *Mezzo Forte* works in a pinch but gets outscaled by late Act 1

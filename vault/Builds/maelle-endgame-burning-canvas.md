# Maelle — Burning Canvas Nuke (endgame)

## Win condition

*Burning Canvas* hits 5 times and consumes accumulated Burn stacks — with ~80 stacks built up by Verso the turn before, each Canvas hit can exceed 15 M damage. Routine numbers: **75 million damage** on a single cast, enough to eliminate most bosses before their first turn.

## Rotation

| Turn | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 0 | Verso applies Burn stacks during his turn |
| 1 (Maelle) | *Swift Stride* into Virtuose, then *Burning Canvas* — detonation turn |
| cleanup | *Fleuret Fury* or *Phantom Strike* on the survivor |

## Stat priority

**Def > Agi > Might**. Defence scales the Painted Power value once At Death's Door is active, Agility for the turn order and Swift Stride, Might is trivial — the damage comes from Burn multiplication, not weapon scaling.

## Variants

- **Esqium** late game consumes AP for massive flat damage on Burning Canvas — replaces Medalum as the primary weapon
- *Brulerum* or *Lithum* in a pinch

# Maelle — Stance DPS

## Win condition

Maelle doesn't need setup turns — her stance changes happen inline with offensive skills. Triple-Powerful makes every turn hit above baseline without any Gradient or Stain prep.

## Rotation

- Stay in **Virtuose stance** against single targets; **Agile stance** against fast-hitting AoE.
- Every turn, your first offensive skill fires with Powerful ×1.875 effective (×1.5 base × ×1.25 Greater × Auto refresh).
- Secondary hits still benefit from pictos and crit.

## Why A-tier

No ramp-up, no gating. Great for the **first attempt** on a new boss where you don't yet know the windows for Overcharge-style burst. The ceiling is lower than Gustave-Overcharge but the floor is much higher.

## Variants

- On fights where stance swaps cost too much tempo, drop Auto-Powerful for *Alternating Critical*

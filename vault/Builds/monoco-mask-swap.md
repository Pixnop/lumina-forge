# Monoco — Mask swap

## Win condition

Monoco transforms into defeated enemies, inheriting part of their kit. The build exploits this by keeping a handful of offensive masks rotating, with Powerful stacking to amplify whatever the current mask outputs.

## Rotation

- Early turns: collect 2-3 masks from standard enemies.
- Boss turn: swap to the most offensive mask available, fire its signature skill with Powerful active.
- Between waves, Augmented Counter I rewards proactive dodging for a modest burst.

## Why A-tier

Ceiling is high (some masks are genuinely broken — e.g. flame-beast masks with built-in burn) but consistency varies fight-to-fight. Works better once you've filled his mask collection.

## Synergies

- [[Synergies/powerful-triple]] — consistent damage floor regardless of mask
- [[Synergies/dodge-counter]] — secondary for patient players

## Variants

- Drop Augmented Counter I for Auto-Powerful if you want purely passive play
- On fights where a specific boss has a god-tier mask (boss-knockoff), swap in element-specific pictos to amplify that element

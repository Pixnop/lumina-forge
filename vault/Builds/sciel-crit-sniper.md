# Sciel — Crit Sniper

## Win condition

40 Agility + 25 Luck ≈ 26% crit rate, with Alternating Critical guaranteeing every 2nd hit crits anyway. Each crit applies a Burn stack via Critical Burn; within 2 rotations Burn is capped and every hit is pushing ×1.80+ crit damage with ×1.50 Burn multiplier on top.

## Rotation

- Basic bow attack (multi-hit) → triggers Alternating Critical every other hit → Burn applies fast.
- Once the target is burning hard, swap to Sciel's dedicated crit-multi-hit skill for the burst turn.
- Cards (Sciel's signature mechanic) are played at the end of each cycle for a free +crit damage boost.

## Scaling

- **Agility 40** is the ceiling where marginal crit rate starts tapering.
- Watch your crit damage multiplier — with Augmented Critical picto + lumina you're at ×1.80 base before kit bonuses.

## Synergies

- [[Synergies/crit-stacking]] — the core loop
- [[Synergies/burn-stain-chain]] — layered bonus, especially against fire-stained targets
- [[Synergies/gradient-economy]] — Charging Critical lumina gives +1 Gradient per crit, keeping the bar full

## Variants

- Swap Critical Burn for *Critical Break* on Shield-heavy enemies (Critical Break consumes a Shield stack for a damage burst)

# Sciel — Lone Wolf (early)

## Win condition

Stack Luck + Agility to reach **100 % crit rate**. With every hit crit, *Focused Foretell* never gets stripped, you maintain the foretell damage bonus through the entire fight, and *Phantom Blade* becomes a reliable break-then-nuke finisher.

## Rotation

| Turn | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | *Focused Foretell* — lock in the damage bonus |
| 2+ | *Sealed Fate* / *Marking Card* for consistent crit pressure |
| burst | *Phantom Blade* to break + burst a priority target |

## Stat priority

**Luck > Agility > Might**. The build lives or dies on 100 % crit rate — once that's secured, Agility gives you turn-order control, Might is pure gravy.

## Variants

- *Chation* works if *Scieleson* doesn't drop
- Swap *Breaking Counter* into the lumina pool vs armoured bosses

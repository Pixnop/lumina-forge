# Sciel — Support (endgame)

## Win condition

Sciel doesn't top the DPS charts here — she **doubles Maelle's damage** via *Fortune's Fury* and **advances ally turns** via Auto Rush + Greater Rush so the party's first-strike advantage snowballs.

## Rotation

| Turn | Action |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | *Fortune's Fury* on Maelle — doubles her next attack's damage |
| 2 | *Intervention* to save a low ally or clear a debuff |
| 3 | *Marking Card* to stack Mark on the target Maelle is about to nuke |

## Stat priority

**Agi > Luck**. Agility lets Sciel go first and line up the buffs before Maelle's turn. Luck keeps crit pressure up for Critical Break procs.

## Variants

- Swap in *Death Bomb* on fights where you can afford one death to trigger massive AoE
- *Breaking Death* replaces *Shielding Death* if the party already runs a reliable revive

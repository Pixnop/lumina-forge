# Verso — Dualist

## Win condition

Verso's dual-wield attacks produce 4-8 individual hits per skill. Alternating Critical guarantees every 2nd hit crits → 2-4 guaranteed crits per skill → massive expected DPS even at moderate underlying crit rate.

Counter I + reactive dodging produces a turn-after burst on top.

## Rotation

- Active dodges whenever possible — Counter I procs on the follow-up, Augmented Critical stacks nudge the damage per hit past the crit threshold.
- Between dodges, unload whatever dual-wield skill is off cooldown.

## Scaling

- Balanced Might/Agility — Verso scales from both on most weapons.
- **Don't push Vitality above 10** on this build; his defensive play comes from dodges, not HP pool.

## Synergies

- [[Synergies/crit-stacking]] — core
- [[Synergies/dodge-counter]] — secondary; rewards reactive play

## Variants

- Swap Counter I for *Critical Burn* picto if you prefer a pure DPS build with less dodge dependency
- *Blodam* over Noahram on fights where Agility scaling beats Might

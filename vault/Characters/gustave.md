Gustave is a resourceful and dedicated Engineer who volunteers to join Expedition 33. He devotes his final year to defeating the Paintress and to reclaim the future for the people of Lumiere. His worldview has been shaped by having lived his entire life with the threat of the Paintress' presence over Lumière. He is both the foster father and brother of Maelle.

You begin the game playing as Gustave, making your way out of the forest with the overwhelming feeling of hopelessness. Until you find Lune, she encourages you and joins you on your journey.

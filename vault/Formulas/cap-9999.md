# The 9999 cap

Every individual hit is clamped to 9999 damage in normal play. This is a hard ceiling — any formula output above is simply `min(9999, dmg)`.

## Consequences for build choice

- Multi-hit skills (3-6 hits per use) beat single-hit finishers once you approach the cap, because each hit caps independently.
- Past ~Might 80 + heavy picto stacking, adding more offense does nothing for single-hit builds.
- Crit damage scaling is still useful below cap, but loses value once hits routinely hit 9999.

## Scorer behaviour

The optimizer **does apply the cap** when scoring. That means two builds with 12k and 25k raw DPS will score differently on single-hit weapons (12k → 9999, 25k → 9999, so they tie at the cap) but differ on a 3-hit weapon (3×9999 vs. 3×9999 → same again; the gap only opens up if the first build has fewer hits).

Effectively, once a build caps, the scorer reaches for multi-hit pictos/weapons and crit consistency over raw damage multipliers.

## Bypassing

See [[painted-power]] — the only mechanic that lifts the cap.

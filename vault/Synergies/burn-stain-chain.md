# Burn + Stain chain

## How the combo works

1. Stack crit rate via Agility / Luck / pictos.
2. **Critical Burn** picto applies Burn on every critical hit — effectively 1-2 stacks per turn at 50%+ crit rate.
3. After 2-3 turns, Burn hits the x1.50 cap on the target.
4. Fire an elemental skill that matches a Stain (Fire, Ice, Lightning — character-dependent) to consume the Stain for ×1.50 damage.
5. Meanwhile the target keeps taking per-turn burn ticks.

## Why it's strong

Status effects multiply **on top of** the picto multiplier. A build that already pushes ×1.728 via three offensive pictos becomes ×1.728 × 1.50 × 1.50 ≈ ×3.89 when both Burn-cap and Stain trigger on the same hit.

## Counterplay

- Boss enemies with **Burn Immunity** (e.g. certain late-game fire-native bosses) nuke the combo. Check `vault/Bosses` (future scrape) before committing.
- AoE rotations suffer — Stain is single-target.

# Dodge counter

## How it works

Each **Augmented Counter** picto (I/II/III) adds a damage bonus on the turn right after a successful dodge. They stack:

- Counter I: +25% on the counter hit
- Counter II: +50% total if the previous turn also dodged
- Counter III: +100% total if three dodges in a row (rare but massive)

## Why B-tier

Excellent **ceiling** — the ×2.00 Counter III multiplier is bigger than almost any single buff. But the **floor** is zero: if the player isn't hitting dodges consistently, all three picto slots are wasted.

## Best for

- High-skill play or specific boss fights with predictable wind-ups
- Verso's dodge-roll kit takes natural advantage
- Any character with a Defense-scaling weapon so the defensive stats aren't wasted

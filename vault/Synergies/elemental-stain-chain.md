# Elemental stain chain

## How it works

1. Equip an elemental weapon (Fire for Gustave's *From Fire*, Storm for *Strike Storm*, etc.).
2. Turn 1: cast an elemental skill → applies an elemental Stain (e.g. Fire Stain).
3. Turn 2: cast a **different** elemental skill → consumes the Stain (×1.50) AND applies a new Stain of that element.
4. Cycle.

## Why it works

Unlike Burn-Stain chain which needs 2-3 turns to ramp, this kicks in from turn 2. The cost: you have to equip an elemental weapon and know which skills apply which Stain.

## Required

- **Elemental Amplifier** picto — doubles the elemental bonus from ×1.50 to effectively ×1.80 on Stain consumption
- At least 2 elemental skills for the character — most characters have at least Fire and one other

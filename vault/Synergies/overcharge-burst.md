# Overcharge burst

The archetypal Gustave rotation. Nothing in the game one-shots bosses harder than a fully-loaded Overcharge → Lumiere combo.

## Turn-by-turn

| Turn | Action | State after |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | *Overcharge* | +Powerful next skill, +Gradient 3 next turn |
| 2 | *Lumiere Assault* | damage ×1.50 (Powerful) × ×1.75 (Gradient 3) × picto stack |
| 3 | refresh with *From Fire* or basic attack | rebuild Gradient |

## Damage math (example)

Noahram 100 base × 3 hits × 1.50 Might × 1.16 pictos × 1.10 luminas × 2.625 (Powerful × Gradient 3) ≈ **1500+ per hit** on turn 2. With Critical Burn + Augmented Critical stacking on top this routinely reaches the 9999 cap.

## Required components

- **Gradient Overcharge** picto — guarantees Gradient 3 next turn after using Overcharge
- **Empowering Jumps** — AP economy picto so Gustave can afford 3 AP Overcharge + 2 AP Lumiere on the same turn cycle
- **Powerful Attack** — amplifies the Lumiere hit above and beyond the free Powerful stack

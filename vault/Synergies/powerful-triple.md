# Powerful triple-stack

## How it works

- **Powerful Attack** — next skill does ×1.50 damage. Consumed on use.
- **Greater Powerful** — Powerful hits do ×1.25 extra (so Powerful ≈ ×1.875 effective).
- **Auto-Powerful** — start of turn, gain Powerful if you don't have it. Free uptime.

Combined, every offensive turn benefits from a guaranteed Powerful trigger at ×1.875.

## Why A-tier not S-tier

Less bursty than Overcharge-Burst or Crit-stacking — it's a flat ×1.875 multiplier that always works, which is great for **beginners** or characters without a signature burst pattern.

## Best characters

- **Maelle** — stance-based so she can't afford lots of setup turns; Auto-Powerful guarantees damage without prep.
- **Monoco** — similar reasoning, mask-swap rotations leave little room for Overcharge-style prep.
